About

Welcome to Data Science Society, W&M's Data Science Club! This organization was established to provide an all-inclusive environment for data enthusiasts (like you!) to learn something new. No previous technical experience is required to join our club! We encourage students from all majors to join. If you are someone who loves learning something new, you're in the right place!

Meetings are held biweekly. Please join the GroupMe to keep updated with any changes and special events. Looking forward to meeting you!
