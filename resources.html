Free Resources:

  • FreeCodeCamp - Get Free certs and Programming Projects!
  • WWCode - Massive library of Video tutorials + Job Board; international organization to support women in STEM
  • GISCorps - Volunteer and Contribute to HOT/OSM (OpenStreetMap) Mapping Projects!
  • Forage - Excellent resource to get to know a company's values & what an employee’s work is like. Get a certificate after completing each virtual work experience!
  • The Odin Project - Learn web development basics, full stack JS and Ruby on Rails

Note: We are seeking to make this a more exhaustive list of free learning resources. If you have suggestions to add to this list, please reach out to Arianna via email (arlagamma@wm.edu) with 'DSS Resources' in the subject line.
